Frequently used TOEFL oral short sentences


Created on 2019 / 07 / 11, 16:28:21#English  #TOEFL  

Some of frequently used oral short sentences in TOEFL examination. Reocrding here for further recap and exercise in the future. Totally about 800+ items without classification.

Group 1

  1. Not yet! 还没！
  2. So long. 再见（过于怀旧）。
  3. Cheer up! 振作起来！
  4. This is my treat. 这次我请客。
  5. Sorry, I decline. 抱歉，我拒绝（婉拒）。
  6. Slow down. 慢点。
  7. Watch out! 当心！
  8. Bottoms up! 干杯（见底那种）！
  9. I doubt it. 我表示怀疑。
  10. Keep it up! 坚持下去！
  11. They hurt! 疼（伤口）！
  12. Never mind. 不要紧。
  13. That’s all. 就这样（表总结）。
  14. Time is up. 时间快到了。
  15. Count me on. 算我一个。
  16. He is my age. 他和我同岁。
  17. Help yourself. 别客气，随便吃。
  18. I’m on a diet. 我在节食。
  19. May I ask who’s calling? 请问是哪位（打电话）？
  20. You set me up! 你出卖我！
  21. Give me a hand! 帮我个忙！
  22. I just made it! 我做到了！
  23. I’ll see to it. 我会留意的。
  24. I’m in a hurry. 我赶时间。
  25. It’s her field. 这是她的本行。
  26. You own me one! 你欠我一个人情!
  27. Any day will do. 哪一天都行。
  28. I don’t mean it. 我不是有意的。
  29. So far, so good. 到目前为止还不错。
  30. He is ill in bed. 他卧病在床。
  31. How’s everything? 一切如何？
  32. I’m on your side. 我站在你这边。
  33. No pain, no gain. 不劳无获。
  34. Well, it depends. 哦，这得看情况而定。
  35. We’re all for it. 我们全都同意。
  36. What a good deal! 真便宜。
  37. You asked for it. 你自讨苦吃。
  38. You have my word. 我保证。
  39. Don’t count on me. 别指望我。
  40. Don’t fall for it! 别上当！
  41. Don’t let me down! 别让我失望！
  42. I beg your pardon? 请您在说一遍？
  43. Just wait and see! 等着瞧！
  44. Make up your mind! 做个决定吧！
  45. Here comes the bus. 汽车来了。
  46. Don’t be so modest! 别谦虚了！
  47. Don’t give me that! 少来这套！
  48. Any messages for me? 有我的留言吗？
  49. Forget it! 休想。
  50. Let me see. 让我想想。

Group 2

  1. I can’t help it anymore! 我再也忍不了了！
  2. It’s a long story. 说来话长。
  3. You can’t miss it! 你一定能找到。
  4. I’ll check it out. 我去查查看。
  5. I can’t follow you. 我不懂你说的。
  6. I felt sort of ill. 我感觉有点不适。
  7. It’s going too far. 太离谱了。
  8. It is growing cool. 天气渐渐凉爽起来。
  9. The answer is zero. 白忙了。
  10. He can hardly speak. 他几乎说不出话来。
  11. He always talks big. 他总是吹牛。
  12. Just read it for me. 就读给我听好了。
  13. Move out of my way! 让开!
  14. Time is running out. 没时间了。
  15. You did fairly well! 你干得相当不错！
  16. Clothes make the man. 人要衣装。
  17. Don’t lose your head. 不要惊慌失措。
  18. He can’t take a joke. 他开不得玩笑。
  19. It’s against the law. 这是违法的。
  20. My mouth is watering (to see so much delicious food). 我要流口水了。
  21. You are just in time. 你来得正是时候。
  22. You need to work out. 你需要去运动锻炼一下。
  23. Don’t be so childish. 别这么孩子气。
  24. Don’t trust to chance! 不要碰运气。
  25. This house is my own. 这所房子是我自己的。
  26. He paused for a reply. 他停下来等着回答。
  27. How much does it cost? 多少钱？
  28. I caught the last bus. 我赶上了最后一班车。
  29. Make yourself at home. 请不要拘礼。
  30. Not a sound was heard. 一点声音也没有。
  31. That’s always the case. 习以为常了。
  32. The road divides here. 这条路在这里分岔。
  33. You are a chicken. 你是个胆小鬼。
  34. My car needs washing. 我的车需要洗一洗。
  35. I’ll have to try that. 我得试试这么做。
  36. He suggested a picnic. 他建议搞一次野餐。
  37. He was not a bit tired. 他一点也不累。
  38. It rather surprised me. 那事使我颇感惊讶。
  39. Let bygones be bygones. 过去的，就让它过去吧。
  40. First come first served. 先到先得。
  41. Great minds think alike. 英雄所见略同。
  42. He has a sense of humor. 他有幽默感。
  43. I develop films myself. 我自己冲洗照片。
  44. I felt no regret for it. 对这件事我不觉得后悔。
  45. I meet the boss himself. 我见到了老板本人。
  46. I owe you for my dinner. 我欠你晚餐的钱。
  47. I’m fed up with my work! 我对工作烦死了!
  48. It’s no use complaining. 发牢骚没什么用。
  49. She’s under the weather. 她心情不好。
  50. The rumor had no basis. 那谣言没有根据。

Group 3

  1. They praised him highly. 他们大大地表扬了他。
  2. He grasped both my hands. 他紧握住我的双手。
  3. I have the right to know. 我有权知道。
  4. I heard some one laughing. 我听见有人在笑。
  5. I’ll just play it by ear. 我到时随机应变。
  6. I suppose you dance much. 我想你常常跳舞吧。
  7. I’m not used to drinking. 我不习惯喝酒。
  8. It’s too good to be true! 好得难以置信。
  9. Let’s not waste our time. 咱们别浪费时间了。
  10. Neither of the men spoke. 两个人都没说过话。
  11. Stop making such a noise. 别吵了。
  12. They crowned him king. 他们拥立他为国王。
  13. They’re in red and white. 他们穿着红白相间的衣服。
  14. What shall we do tonight? 我们今天晚上去干点儿什么呢？
  15. You mustn’t aim too high. 你不可好高骛远。
  16. You’ve got a point there. 你说得挺有道理的。
  17. Don’t cry over spilt milk. 不要做无益的后悔。
  18. Don’t let chances pass by. 不要让机遇从我们身边溜走。
  19. He owned himself defeated. 他承认自己失败了。
  20. He seems at little nervous. 他显得有点紧张。
  21. He strolls about the town. 他在镇上四处遛达。
  22. How about a drink tonight? 今晚喝一杯怎样？
  23. I get hold of you at last. 我终于找到你了。
  24. I saw it with my own eyes. 我亲眼所见。
  25. No spitting on the street. 禁止在大街上吐痰。
  26. She was totally exhausted. 她累垮了。
  27. That’s the latest fashion. 这是最流行的款式。
  28. There go the house lights. 剧院的灯光灭了。
  29. They are paid by the hour. 他们按时取酬。
  30. We are all busy with work. 我们都忙于工作。
  31. A barking dog doesn’t bite! 吠犬不咬人。
  32. Be careful not to fall ill. 注意不要生病了。
  33. Brevity is the soul of wit. 简洁是智慧的精华。
  34. Don’t dream away your time. 不要虚度光阴。
  35. Don’t keep me waiting long. 不要让我等得太久。
  36. He has quite a few friends. 他有不少的朋友。
  37. He is capable of any crime. 他什么样的坏事都能干得出来。
  38. He walks with a quick pace. 他快步走路。
  39. He was not a little tired. 他很累。
  40. His looks are always funny. 他的样子总是滑稽可笑。
  41. I was taking care of Sally. 我在照顾萨莉。
  42. I wish I lived in New York. 我希望住在纽约。
  43. I’m your lucky fellow then. 我就是你的幸运舞伴啦！
  44. No littering on the campus. 在校园内不准乱丢废物。
  45. She mended the broken doll. 她修补了破了的洋娃娃。
  46. The figure seems all right. 数目看起来是对的。
  47. We walk on the garden path. 我们走在花园小径上。
  48. A lost chance never returns. 错过的机会永不再来。
  49. Don’t let this get you down. 不要为此灰心丧气。
  50. I haven’t seen you for ages. 我好久没见到你了。

Group 4

  1. It’s very thoughtful of you. 你想得真周到。
  2. Opportunity knocks but once. 机不可失，时不再来。
  3. She dressed herself hastily. 她匆忙穿上衣服。
  4. She hired a car by the hour. 她租了一辆按钟点计费的汽车。
  5. Someone is ringing the bell. 有人在按门铃。
  6. These shoes don’t fit right. 这双鞋不太合适。
  7. This is only the first half. 这才是上半场呢。
  8. This pen doesn’t write well. 这钢笔不好写。
  9. You really look sharp today. 你今天真漂亮。
  10. Don’t keep the truth from me. 别瞒着我事实真相。
  11. He came to the point at once. 他一下子就说到了点子上。
  12. He fell behind with his work. 他工作落后了。
  13. He is the happiest man alive. 他是世界上最快乐的人。

Misc

  1. Personally, I think that …
  2. If it were up to me, I would …
  3. They’ll probably …
  4. I’ll have a glass of wine please.
  5. Sorry, what did you say?
  6. You are an excellent cook!
  7. What’s the magic word? … please.
  8. Truth or Dare.
