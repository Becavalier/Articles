Publication

Book《深入浅出 WebAssembly》(First Edition)

2018.11

Introduction:This book strives to start with some simple practices, then deep dive into the theory. It introduces the development detail of WebAssembly technology, and various underlying design principles and implementations, related toolchains and future developing directions and many other aspects. The overall writing time is more than 9 months.

Content:The development path of WebAssembly technology, from PNaCl to ASM.js to Wasm, and the primary application methods and performance comparison of these technologies; Wasm standard client-based API, the design principle of the underlying stack machine, and an in-depth interpretation of the MVP standard theory; Advanced content related to Wasm standards, such as single instruction multiple data stream (SIMD), dynamic link (DL), etc. LLVM toolchain and WAT readable text format; the necessary process of developing Wasm applications based on Emscripten toolchain, and also some essential functionalities and features of the toolchain; C/C++ language dynamic relationship binding technology based on the Emscripten toolchain; some other advanced features such as WebGL support, virtual file system, application optimization, and HTML5 event system provided by the Emscripten toolchain, the current development status of the Wasm ecosystem, and some of the Wasm future development features that were already designed in the Post-MVP standard.
